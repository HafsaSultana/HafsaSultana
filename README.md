## 💫 About Me

Hey! I'm **Hafsa Sultana** — an AI Engineer and NLP enthusiast from **Bangladesh 🇧🇩**, passionate about building intelligent systems that actually make a difference.

I spend most of my time working with **Transformers, LLMs, and Bangla NLP** — trying to make AI more accessible for low-resource languages and educational contexts. My current obsession is **question generation from Bangla text**, and I'm diving deeper into **RAG pipelines** and **AI evaluation systems** every day.

I believe the best AI isn't just technically impressive — it's useful, explainable, and built for real people.

🔭 &nbsp;**Currently building** — Bangla NLP systems, LLM-powered chatbots, and AI evaluation pipelines  
🌱 &nbsp;**Currently learning** — AI Agentic system, LLM fine-tuning, RAG architecture, and MLOps  
💡 &nbsp;**Interested in** — AI in education, low-resource NLP, and Generative AI research
